AboutUs

The theme of our resturant is all space related, with all of our resturants we aim to have unique aspects with all of our resturants covering different planets to different galaxy. We also have VR experiences within our resturants for customers to enjoy and learn more about space.

Within our resturant we hope to provide our customer with a high quality service that makes them feel at home but also they have left eart hto enjoy a new experience.

Our ingredience are all from local areas, offering all our customers high quality food made by very experienced chefs. To ensure there is no waste within our resturants our food is prepared and cooked on order, this is to ensure that the products can remain fresh. We also ensure that all of the foods and drinks on our menu contains the ingredience.

Providing great food for our customers is very important within our resturants, to ensure we do this all food is checked on delivery to ensure the quality is up to standard from our quality check list.
